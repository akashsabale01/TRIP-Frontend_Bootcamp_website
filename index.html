Frequently Asked Questions

Trip Frontend Camp is one of a kind Career Program designed to enhance your Technical skills. It takes you from being a Beginner to Job-ready in 9 months, and send you off on a path of a better job and higher earnings. Note this is open for Final Year Students and Graduates.

The Duration of the course is 21 months. The first 9 months will be focussing on Learning & the next 12 months on getting you placed.

We expect you to have good aptitude skills which show your problem-solving ability. We also test your basic programming skills to understand the level of Coding experience you have and create your learning path accordingly.

Our Standard plan is of Rs 5000.

We will create your profile, prepare a resume, conduct mock interviews and do our best to help you land up in a Tech job.
